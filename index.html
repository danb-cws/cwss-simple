I do stuff like...

  • Styling: CSS/SASS/PostCSS, responsive/adaptive layouts, grid and flexbox, CSS animation, white labelling considerations, SVG, pattern library development, browser degradation. I love pairing with UX/designers on 'Rapid prototyping' style, in-browser development via dev tools and a well thought out cascade of configs and inheriting SASS variables.
